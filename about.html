ADVANCEMANTS

GTB Steel Corporation (Pvt) Ltd., is an industry leader acclaimed for introducing Thermo Mechanically Treated (TMT) reinforcement steel manufacturing to Sri Lanka. Established in 1999 as one of the largest BOI ventures, we commenced manufacturing and marketing TMT steel bars of profoundly superior quality.


The technological advancements of TMT steel bars manufactured by CTB" Steel ensure distinct. advantages including higher bond strength, excellent bendability, superior weldability, higher corrosion resistance, superior seismic resistance and many more.


GTB Steel is an ISO 9001: 2015 certified organization, recognised for its quality management system. Our plant has a capacity of manufacturing upto 9.000 MT a month. Forging ahead with new technological advancements, our products provide a superior level of core strength needed to meet increasing demands and complexities of engineering and construction industries of tomorrow and beyond.
