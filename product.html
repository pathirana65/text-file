GTB TMT 500 Advantage

Having redefined steel manufacturing with the introduction of TMT technology to Sri Lanka, GTR Steel adapted cutting edge Thermo Mechanical Treatment Process (THERMEX) developed by QEK. Germany in manufacturing High Strength Deformed Bars that conform to the requirements of civil engineering needs. The technically superior THERMEX process ensures strength, actility, weldability and elongation values consistently with every bar CTB Steel manufactures, providing more value and fulfilling the evolving dynamics of structural engineering requirements.


The strength of TMT bars is profoundly regulated by optimizing the water pressure of the pearlitic core and the rough surface of the tempered martensite of them. As a result, optimum strength, ductility and toughness is provided to the TMT bars


Due to these advantages TMT bars are widely used in concrete reinforcement structures, industrial structures, high-rise buildings, underground platforms in metro rallway and rapid transport systems, bridges, flyovers, dams, thermal and hydro power plants.
