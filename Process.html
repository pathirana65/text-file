Our Cutting-Edge Process


Our Cutting-Edge Process Microprocessor-based. Programmable Logical Controlled (PLC) re-heating furnace is used for heating billets sourced from prime steel mills and then rolled through a series of mill stands, progressively reducing the section to end size and shape range to produce bars at controlled temperatures Subjecting to THERMEX Thermomechanical Process immediately alter the bars leave their final stand while achieving the desired result of finished CTB TMT 500 steel bars of profound quality.
